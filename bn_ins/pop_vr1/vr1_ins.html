VR1 Regulator for Radiant Heater control

FEATURES

  • Suitable for up to 3KW radiant heater at 240V supply
  • Integral ON/OFF switch
  • High reliability semiconductor switch
  • Integral RFI Filter for CE EMC requirement
  • Low cost & compact

VR1 manual control phase angle energy regulator is a compact, low cost unit for controlling the power from 0% to 100% into a 240V radiant heater. To reduce stress and increase reliability it uses a most advanced 25A, high surge rating triac.

Notes

INSTALLATION AND OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS

A VR1 should be connected according to the following wiring diagram. The size of cables to L, N, E and the load must be sufficient to withstand the maximum current rating of the infra red heater and meet the IEE WIRING REGULATIONS.

The VR1 must be mounted proud of a wall and not recessed into a cavity.

Protection Fuse

It is recommended to use a suitable fuse or circuit breaker (MCB) to protect a VR1 and the supply from overloading. The fuse level depends on the load current rating. The maximum fuse value for a VR1 is 20A.
