BN Thermic Instruction Sheet

HT Tripod Mounted Halogen Heater

The HT is supplied as a three part unit - Heater Body - Switch bracket and cable, plus a separate Tripod stand, ready for quick and easy assembly

Step 1 - The switch bracket should be attached to the heater body by means of the bolt supplied, ensuring the spacer provided is fitted between the two fixing posts of the heater. Please ensure the star washer is fitted under the head of the bolt, then fully tighten to secure.

Step 2 - When fitted, the complete heater and switch bracket can be secured to the top of the tripod, using the small hand-wheel fixing screw provided.

Step 3 - The tripod can be height adjusted up and down by loosening and tightening the securing rings on the tripod.

Always unplug this appliance before removing any cover or guard, also before any cleaning or maintenance is carried out

Reflectors

The reflectors should be kept clean at all times to ensure maximum heat reflection. Dirty reflectors will reduce the energy transmitted and seriously affect the heat output.

Infrared Lamps/Emitters

Before attempting to change or replace the lamp/emitter in your heater, first check the power supply has not been interrupted. If the illuminated on/off main switch on the side of the cassette is illuminated and switched on, but the unit is still not working, you will need to change the lamp/emitter.

SHOULD IT BE NECESSARY TO CHANGE A LAMP/EMITTER -PLEASE FOLLOW THESE INSTRUCTIONS.

TURN HEATER OFF AT MAIN SUPPLY AND REMOVE PLUG, Then remove both end reflectors of the heater and guard, exposing the connection terminals. Release the 2 nuts at each end and remove the fork terminals the lamp can now be released by unclipping it from the fitting.

Replace with a new lamp (same type, make and model), ensuring connections are tight.

Replace end reflectors and guard, plug-in and switch on.

If for any reason the machine still fails to operate, please contact your local dealer/supplier for advice.

Please do not attempt to carry out any other form of maintenance or electrical adjustments to this heater.

TO KEEP YOUR HEATLIGHT TECHNOLOGY EQUIPMENT IN GOOD WORKING ORDER - ALWAYS KEEP THE UNIT CLEAN AND STORED IN A DRY PLACE.

Safety Tips

220/240V models - A 13amp three-pin plug with earth connection (fitted ) must always be used with this appliance.

100/120V models - A 15amp three-pin plug with earth connection (fitted ) must always be used with this appliance.

Please check rating label to ensure correct VOLTAGE before connection to any mains supply.

When in use, please observe a Static Safety Distance of 1000mm (36inches) between the heater and any object.

Always unplug before removing any cover or guard, also before cleaning or maintenance is carried out.

Always switch off and unplug the unit before moving the tripod.
