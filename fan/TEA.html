TEA

Portable fan assisted unit heaters

Application

The TEA range of portable unit heaters provides space heating in infrequently used areas. These heaters are commonly used in workshops and for drying applications.

The TEA is a space heater designed to heat large volumes of air by recirculation. It is not suitable for use as a ‘spot’ heater.

Features

  • Stainless steel sheathed elements for long life
  • Heavy duty construction with white (RAL 9016) epoxy finish
  • 2m lead and suitable plug (no plug fitted to 32kW heater)
  • Over temperature protection
  • On/off/fan only settings
  • Built-in adjustable thermostat
  • Three heat selection on larger models
  • Ingress protection to IP44
