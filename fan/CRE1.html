CRE1

Corrosion resistant washdown unit heaters

Application

The CRE1 range of unit heaters is designed to provide background or comfort heat in damp or corrosive environments, the heaters are suitable for washdown (maximum water pressure 480kPaG). Typical applications include water treatment plants, car and commercial vehicle washes and chemical plants.

Features

  • Ingress protection to IP55
  • 16-guage stainless steel cabinet
  • Stainless steel wall/ceiling mounting kit
  • Auto-reset over temperature protection
