DFF

Downflow fans

DFF is not a heater. It is used in conjuction with other heating systems.

Application

The DFF range of downflow fans is designed to recycle warm stratified air, returning it to working level. These units are widely used in industrial premises with large air volumes and high roof levels. Typically they would be used in conjunction with BN Thermic OUH unit heaters.

Selection

  • Refer to table and select model depending on height of the building
  • Calculate volume (m3) of unused space (typically above eaves)
  • Calculate the quantity of units required by multiplying volume of unused space (m3) by the number of air changes per hour in the space you are heating (typically between 1 and 3 for industrial spaces) and dividing the answer by the airflow (m3/h) of selected model

Features

  • Robust steel case with black epoxy coating (RAL9011)
  • Internal adjustable air deflection louvres
  • Built-in adjustable thermostat to detect build up of warm air
  • Four eye hooks to facilitate suspension using chains (not supplied)
