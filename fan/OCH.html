OCH

High capacity fan assisted cabinet heaters

Application

The OCH range of high capacity cabinet heaters is ideally suited for use in public areas such as waiting rooms, stairways and corridors. The OCH is a very versatile heater with 9 different mounting arrangements (floor, wall or ceiling / surface, semi-recessed or fully recessed) and 4 air inlet/outlet configurations.

Features

  • Finned tubular heating elements for long life
  • Air inlet and outlet from front panel - no requirement for air supply to the rear of the unit
  • Auto reset over temperature protection
  • Heavy gauge steel construction
  • Almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
  • Tamperproof ‘on/off’, ‘fan only’ and ‘high/low’ switches (high/low option not available for ceiling mounted heaters)
