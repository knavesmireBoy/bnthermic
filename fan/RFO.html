RFO

Heavy-duty wall mounted fan assisted heaters - surface or recessed

Application

The RFO range can be used as the main source of heat or as supplementary heating in a wide variety of commercial and industrial applications.

The robust steel construction makes it suitable for public areas where wear and tear is inevitable and the recessed option makes it ideal for installation where space is at a premium.

Features

  • Finned tubular heating elements for long life
  • Built-in control thermostat can be made tamperproof
  • Air inlet and outlet from front panel - no requirement for air supply to the rear of the unit
  • Over temperature protection
  • Fan delay to clear residual heat
  • Heavy gauge steel construction
  • Almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
