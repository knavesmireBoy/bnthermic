700 Series

High level or overdoor fan assisted heaters

Application

These stylish fan assisted heaters can be mounted over a door to offset the heat losses associated with frequently opening doors. Alternatively they can be used as general space heaters. Typical applications include shops, cafes, waiting rooms and workshops.

Features

  • Dual function mounting bracket. Vertical discharge for overdoor mounting and adjustable angle discharge for general space heating
  • Attractive white RAL9010 finish
  • 1m lead
  • Autoreset over temperature protection
  • On/off/fan only settings - 730
  • Built-in electronic temperature control - 730T
  • Suitable for use with wall mounted controller - 730R
  • Off/half heat/full heat settings - 745
