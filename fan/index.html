Fan Assisted Heaters

Fan assisted heaters provide a fast and cost effective way of supplying comfort heat in commercial and industrial buildings.

The BN Thermic range includes:
  • Overdoor heaters to offset heat loss associated with doors and entrances
  • Wall or ceiling mounted heaters with outputs up to 25kW for general space heating
  • Recessed heating for walls, ceilings and floors
  • Portable heaters
  • ATEX approved heaters for hazardous areas
