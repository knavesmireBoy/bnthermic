SCH

Fan assisted suspended ceiling heaters

Application

Purpose designed for use in a standard 600 x 600 modular ceiling system.
Typical applications include shops and offices

Features

  • White aluminium 50mm x 50mm egg-crate diffuser supplied as standard
  • Air inlet and outlet from the front panel - no requirement for air supply from ceiling void
  • SCHG-30E has built-in electronic controller - fan operates continuously with controller monitoring ambient temperature
  • Autoreset over temperature protection
  • Rubber fittings for low noise operation
