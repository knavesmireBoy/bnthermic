OUH

Fan assisted unit heaters

Application

Reliable, robust and safe, the OUH range is the simplest way of providing comfort or background heat in commercial or industrial buildings.

The OUH is a space heater designed to heat large volumes of air by recirculation. It is not suitable for use as a ‘spot’ heater.

Features

  • Tubular heating elements for long life
  • Auto reset over temperature protection
  • Heavy gauge steel construction
  • Almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
  • Safety grilles on air inlet and exhaust
  • Fan delay to clear residual heat
  • Individually adjustable directional louvres
  • Wall and ceiling mounting brackets as standard (can be vertically suspended using optional kit)
  • Integral contactor and fused control circuit
