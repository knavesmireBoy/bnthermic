WHE

Compact wall mounted fan assisted heaters - surface or recessed

Application

The small size and versatile mounting arrangements make the WHE range ideal for use crane cabs, toll booths or similar areas where space is limited.

Features

  • Finned tubular heating elements for long life
  • Air inlet and outlet from front panel - no requirement for air supply to the rear of the unit
  • Auto reset over temperature protection (one model with manual reset device)
  • Heavy gauge steel construction
  • Almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
  • 110V model available
