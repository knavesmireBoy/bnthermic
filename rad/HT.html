HT-2000

HT

Tripod mounted portable halogen heater

Application

Halogen heaters emit short wave radiant energy that passes through the air with minimal losses and is converted to warmth when absorbed by a solid body. They deliver 100% of their output instantly. HT offers a simple, portable method of providing radiant heat exactly where it is required.

Features

  • On/off switch
  • 4.5m lead with fused 13A plug (not fitted to 110V model)
  • Full directional adjustment with height adjustment between 0.65 and 1.63m
  • Lamp safety guard
