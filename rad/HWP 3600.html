HWP-3600

Weather resistant halogen heater (three-heater assembly)

Application

The HWP-3600 is an attractively mounted cluster of three halogen heaters, each of which provides 1.2kW of instant and direct radiant heat. It has been designed for use in public areas such as pub gardens and smoking areas and can either be floor mounted or suspended from an overhead structure.

Features

  • Each heater has a built-in protective guard
  • Ingress protection to IP65
  • Glass free heater face promotes long life and increased efficiency
  • Individual heater angles easily adjusted
  • Heaters fitted with individual on/off switches and can be individually replaced.
  • Choice of robust, high quality mounting system
  • FM model has sturdy steel base for bolting to concrete and a 2.35 long plated steel pole (supplied in two sections)
  • OH model has a robust socket for bolting to a suitable overhead structure and a 0.52m long plated steel pole.
