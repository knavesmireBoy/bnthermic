Radiant Heaters

Radiant heaters can provide a sophisticated method of supplying comfort heat in both industrial and commercial environments. Alternatively radiant heaters can provide ’spot’ heat in areas such as loading bays.

  • The BN Thermic range includes:
  • Long wave ’black heat’ panels
  • Halogen and ceramic heaters
  • Portable heaters
  • Weatherproof heaters for outdoor use
