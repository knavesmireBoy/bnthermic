HP/CP

Floor standing portable radiant heaters

Application

Halogen heaters emit short wave radiant energy that passes through the air with minimal losses and is converted to warmth when absorbed by a solid body. They deliver 100% of their output virtually instantly. These easily portable floor-standing heaters allow the heat to be directed exactly where it is required. The CP uses ceramic heat emitters and should be used where the light output from a halogen lamp is not acceptable.

Features

  • On/off switch and safety ‘tip over’ switch.
  • 5m lead with fused 13A plug
  • Built-in safety guard
