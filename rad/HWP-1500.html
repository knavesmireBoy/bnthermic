HWP-1500

Weather resistant halogen heater

Application

Halogen heaters emit short wave radiant energy that passes through the air with minimal losses and is converted to warmth when absorbed by a solid body. They deliver 100% of their output the instant they are energised. The HWP is specifically designed for applications such as patios, pub gardens, pavement cafes, golf driving ranges and outdoor smoking areas.

Features

  • Built-in protective guard
  • Ingress protection to IP65
  • Bracket for wall mounting
  • Glass free front face promotes long life and increased efficiency
