Advantages of Radiant Cassettes

Radiant cassettes offer several advantages over traditional convection based heating systems.
  • High level mounting allows flexible use of space
  • Gentle radiant effect means that the ‘experienced temperature’ is somewhat warmer than the actual air temperature operating costs are correspondingly lower.
  • The temperature gradient is very slight which means that the temperature at floor level is virtually the same as at ceiling level.
  • Radiant heating systems do not reply on air movement and thus avoid the draughts and noise associated with convection based systems.

TVH

TVH

Heavy duty radiant cassettes

Application

TVH cassettes are an ideal method of providing space heating in areas with high ceilings such as factories, warehouses, terminal buildings and shopping centres. The cassettes can be mounted at heights between 5m and 20m and, unlike fan assisted heaters, do not give rise to a significant temperature gradient.

When an even distribution of heat is required, ensure that the distance between the cassettes is not greater that the distance between the cassettes and the floor level. Radiant cassettes are designed to raise the air temperature in a confined space. They are not ideal for use as `spot` heaters.
