TP

Thyristor control panels

Application

TP thyristor control panels are designed for use with gradient heating cassettes. They will ensure that a group of heaters delivers the exact percentage of its total output needed to maintain the required temperature. In this way the TP ensures optimum comfort and maximum economy.

Features

  • Epoxy painted steel enclosure conforming to IP21
  • Door interlocked isolator
  • Panel front mounted PID controller
  • Panel front mounted on/off switch and indicators
  • Black bulb PT100 room temperature sensor with 10m cable
  • Built-in cooling fans
