SIM351R
Description: 7 day programmable pilot wire controller specific to EP panel heaters
Mode of operation: The SIM351R will control a group of up to 30 EP panel heaters. The heaters must be connected to the controller by pilot wire. The controller will switch between comfort temperature (as set on the individual heater thermostats) and set-back (4°C below comfort temperature) according to the programme.
Manual overrides: All heaters ‘off’/ constant comfort/ constant set-back/ constant frost protection
Mounting: Wall - surface
Voltage Supply: 230V
Dimensions: 90mm (H) x 150mm (W) x 35mm (D)
Ingress protection: IP30
