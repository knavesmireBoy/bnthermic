Our Product Range

Fan Assisted Heaters
Fan assisted heaters provide a fast and cost effective way of supplying comfort heat in commercial and industrial buildings.
Natural Convection Heaters
Natural convection heaters provide silent heating in a wide variety of commercial and industrial locations.
Radiant Heaters
Radiant heaters can provide a sophisticated method of supplying comfort heat in both industrial and commercial environments. Alternatively radiant heaters can provide "spot" heat in areas such as loading bays.
Underfloor heating
Electric underfloor heating can be used to eliminate the discomfort of cold ceramic or stone floors or to provide a complete heating system.
Pipe freeze protection
Pipe freeze protection cable prevents pipes from freezing during winter months.
Controls
BN Thermic offers a comprehensive range of controls for all five product families.
