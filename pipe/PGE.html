PGE

Pre-terminated water pipe freeze protection cable

Application

PGE cables are a simple way of preventing water pipes from freezing. They should be installed directly onto the pipe under a layer of insulation. PGE cables cannot be cut to length and must not be allowed to touch or cross.

Features

  • Pre-terminated with 2m 3 core cold lead
  • Built-in in-line thermostat energises the cable when temperatures fall below 3°C
  • Metal earth braid running full length of the cable
  • Fully moisture proof
