CF1

ATEX approved EExd cabinet style convector heaters

Application

CF1 heaters can be installed where potentially explosive gasses may be present and are suitable for Zone 1 and Zone 2 hazardous areas and for gas groups IIA and IIB. They must be mounted horizontally and operated where the ambient temperature does not exceed 40ºC. Heaters stamped T3 can be installed in areas designated T3, T2 or T1. Heaters stamped T4 can be installed in areas designa ted T4, T3, T2 or T1.

Typical applications include paint stores and petrochemical installation. Their tough and compact construction makes the CF1 particularly suitable where space is at a premium.

Features

  • ATEX certified
  • Extremely tough 2mm thick epoxy coated steel cabinet with galvanised steel rear panel
  • Galvanised steel brackets for wall mounting
  • Sloped top prevents objects obstructing air outlet
