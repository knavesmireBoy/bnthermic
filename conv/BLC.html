BLC

Low level heaters

Application

When mounted at skirting level, the BLC range offers an unobtrusive method of providing comfort heat in areas such as corridors, meeting

rooms and offices. BLC heaters are also frequently installed under church pews providing an economical method of heating a congregation.

Features

  • Robust steel construction with almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
  • Autoreset over temperature protection
  • Electrical connections may be made at either end of heater
