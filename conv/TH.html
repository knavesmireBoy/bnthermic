TH

Trench heaters

Application

As only the aluminium grille remains visible, TH trench heaters provide an extremely discreet method of combating the downdraught associated with glazed walls and large windows. They can also provide supplementary and in many instances primary space heating.

The heaters can be installed end-on-end to form a continuous heating system where necessary employing 90° corner sections and ‘adjust to length’ unheated dummy sections.

Features

  • Robust construction - designed to take foot traffic
  • 1.2mm thick steel case with black internal finish and levelling feet (-0/+40mm)
  • Rigid anodised aluminium cross blade grille with matching support frame
  • Cool air inlet and warm air discharge through floor level grille - no requirement for air supply to the recessed section of the heater
  • Mineral insulated metal sheathed heating elements protected by a safety mesh
  • Auto reset over temperature protection device with back-up manual reset device
