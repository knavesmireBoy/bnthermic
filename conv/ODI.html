ODI

Draught barrier heaters

Application

ODI draught barriers are designed to combat the downdraught associated with glazed walls and large windows.

Features

  • 2mm thick aluminium front panel mounted on a 1.2mm steel cabinet
  • White (RAL 9010) finish
  • Autoreset over temperature protection
  • Electrical connections may be made at either end of heater
  • Sloped top prevents objects obstructing air outlet
