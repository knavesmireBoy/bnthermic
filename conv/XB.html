XB

ATEX Approved EExd Convector Heaters

Application

XB heaters can be installed where potentially explosive gasses may be present and are suitable for Zone 1 and Zone 2 hazardous areas and for gas groups IIA, IIB and IIC. They must be mounted with the terminal enclosure in the horizontal plane and operated where the ambient temperature does not exceed 40ºC. Heaters stamped T3 can be installed in areas designated T3, T2 or T1. Heaters stamped T4 can be installed in areas designated T4, T3, T2 or T1.

Typical applications include paint stores and petrochemical installations

Features

  • ATEX certified
  • Extended surface area black anodised copper free aluminium heat emitter with
    nickel-plated guard
  • Universal support bracket for wall or floor mounting
  • Slide out terminal block trolley simplifies installation and servicing
