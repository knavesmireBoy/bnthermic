BLS

110V Convector heater

Application

Typically used in site huts, the BLS-110-1000T provides heat by natural convection in areas where a 110V supply is available. It is designed to be wall mounted at low level.

Features

  • Robust steel construction with almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
  • Autoreset over temperature protection
  • 2m pre-wired lead
  • Built-in adjustable thermostat
