SWD

Industrial finned tubular heaters

Application

SWD heaters are particularly suited to damp or corrosive environments. Typical applications include water treatment plants, plant rooms and lift shafts.

Features

  • Welded stainless steel tube with stainless steel cooling fins and mounting bracket
  • Glass fibre reinforced terminal enclosure conforming to IP66 and fitted with M20 cable gland
  • Built-in adjustable thermostat with frost setting (SWD-A only)
