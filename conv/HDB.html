HDB

Heavy duty low level heaters

Application

The HDB’s extremely robust construction makes it ideal for use in public areas such as waiting rooms, reception areas and corridors.

Features

  • 1.6mm thick, steel front panel with almond (RAL 9001) paint finish
  • Autoreset over temperature protection
  • Electrical connections may be made at either end of heater
  • Louvred air intlet and outlet
  • Sloped top prevents objects obstructing air outlet
