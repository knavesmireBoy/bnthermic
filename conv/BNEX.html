BNEX

ATEX approved EExe convector heaters

Application

BNEX heaters can be installed where potentially explosive gasses may be present and are suitable for Zone 1 and Zone 2 hazardous areas and for gas groups IIA, IIB and IIC. They must be mounted horizontally and operated where the ambient temperature does not exceed 40ºC. Heaters stamped T3 can be installed in areas designated T3, T2 or T1. Heaters stamped T4 can be installed in areas designated T4, T3, T2 or T1.

Typical applications include paint stores and petrochemical installation.

Features

  • ATEX certified
  • Integral guard and floor mounting brackets
  • Ingress protection to IP67
