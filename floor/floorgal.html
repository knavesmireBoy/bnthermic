Underfloor heating

Electric underfloor heating can be used to eliminate the discomfort of cold ceramic or stone floors or to provide a complete heating system.

The BN Thermic range includes:
  • Mats and cables for use under stone or ceramic floors
  • A system specially designed for laminate or timber floors
